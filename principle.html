ABOUT ECODESIGN
Principles
All principles set a foundation for our Eco Design System. They will help us to create cohesive and sustainable digital products.

Principles based on Sustainability theme

GREEN DESIGN

Products oriented towards reducing CO2 emissions with design. We believe in designing digital experiences that promote green energy, less energy consumption, inclusiveness, and accessibility.

SCALABILITY

We believe that people should have a high-quality experience that effortlessly scales across platforms. Our product should be scalable on all devices for soothing experiences.
